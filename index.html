Highlander Cow Fun Facts

Highlander Cattle originated in the Scottish Highlands and the Outer Hebrides islands of Scotland and has long horns and a long shaggy coat. Highlander cows were orginally black and these days most highlander cows are brown or ginger. Their fluffy long hair help them survive in tough environment. They are the OLDEST cattle breed in the world and can live up to 20 years.
