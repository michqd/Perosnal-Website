Minion's Banana Bread

Index: Cows | Banana Bread

I love the Minion Movies and I like to bake!
This is a banana bread recipe since minions LOVES bananas.

Measurements Ingredients
3-6 Bananas
1.5 cup Flour
1 tsp Baking Soda
1/2 tsp Salt
3/4 cup Unsalted butter
1 cup Light brown sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla extract

Minion's Banana Bread Recipe

  1. Pre-heat the oven for 350 degrees
  2. Add flour, baking soda, and salt into a bowl
  3. In a stand mixer, add soften butter and brown sugar
  4. Put the stand mixer on medium speed for 2 mintues
  5. Then, add eggs and vanilla extract while mixing
  6. After, add in chopped bananas into the stand mixer
  7. Funnel in flour, baking soda, and salt mixture into the
    stand mixer on low speed until combined
  8. Pour batter into loaf pan
  9. Bake for 45-60 mintues
  10. Enjoy the banana bread

This banana bread is MINION APPROVED

Pro Tip:
To make your banana bread taste even better add dark brown sugar on the top of the batter before baking. The brown sugar will caramelized when baked which creates a tasty crust on the banana bread.
Reheating Intructions:
Mircowave - Cut a slice of banana bread. Place the slice of bread on a plate. Heat it inside the mircowave for 30-40 seconds or until warmed. Enjoy warmed banana bread!
Airfryer - Cut a slice of banana bread. Place inside airfryer at 350 degree fahrenheit for 3-5 mintues or until golden brown. Enjoy warmed banana bread!
